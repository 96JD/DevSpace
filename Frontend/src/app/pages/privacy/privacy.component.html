Privacy Policy

DevSpace takes your privacy very seriously. We do not collect or share any personal information unless you explicitly provide it to us. When you use DevSpace, we may collect certain non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system. This information is used solely for the purpose of improving our service and is not shared with third parties. If you choose to register for DevSpace, we will collect your email address and password. This information is stored securely in our database and is used solely for the purpose of authenticating you and providing you with access to our service. We will never share your email address or password with any third parties. DevSpace uses cookies to provide you with a personalized experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and allow us to remember your preferences and settings. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use our service.
